Instructions: Determine if each of the following pairs of cards is a SET.

  • Two cards are a SET if they are alike in shape, color, or opacity - or share more than one similarity.
  • Two cards are not a SET if they are dissimilar in every characteristic.
Press the Y key if you think the cards form a SET, and the N key if you do not. Try to work as quickly as possible without losing accuracy. If you make a mistake, just keep going. There will be 24 pairs.
